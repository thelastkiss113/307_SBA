Criteria

Criteria for Story Submissions

Genre Description Word Count
Paranormal stories must have ghosts or supernatural occurences. ex: The Shining at least 2000
Monster stories must have at least one inhuman creature ex. Frankenstein, Godzilla, Creature from the Black Lagoon 5000 word count maximum
Historical stories must have at least one historical event, place, or person. ex: The Others, Jack The Ripper at least 2000
Experimental stories must go outside normal narrative conventions. ex: The Lighthouse at least 3000
